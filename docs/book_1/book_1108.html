Summary

Whoever has the data has the ability to extract information promptly and effectively to make pertinent decisions. The premise of this handbook is to empower users and tool developers with a collection of formulas and techniques for data analytics and to serve as a quick reference to keep pertinent formulas within fingertips of readers.
